Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with us.

Do you take bulk school and office orders?
Yes, we take bulk school and office orders with discounts.
What payment methods are accepted?
Cash, UPI, and cheque are accepted at the store.
Do you offer local delivery?
No, only store pickup is available.
Can I exchange damaged items?
Yes, sure.
Do you stock specific school board books (like NCERT or State Board)?
No, we do not stock specific board-specific textbooks at this time.
Can I pre-order books that are currently out of stock?
Yes, we allow pre-orders for books that are temporarily unavailable.
Do you offer discounts for students or teachers?
Yes, we offer special discounts for bulk purchases made by students or educators.
Do you provide gift wrapping for stationery items?
No, we do not provide a gift-wrapping service at this moment.
Can I request specific office supplies that aren't usually in your shop?
Yes, we are happy to special order specific office essentials for our customers.
What is your return policy?
Items can be returned if they are undamaged, unused, and accompanied by a valid reason.
